Settlement

Paid requests settle in USDC on Solana. Settlement is final once confirmed on-chain. Refunds are not automated; contact support for disputes.

During preview, Meterflow is offered without an SLA. We aim for high availability and will publish operational status as the dashboard matures.

Termination
