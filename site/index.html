Meter. Control. Account.

Three steps to turn an endpoint, MCP tool, or data feed into a paid agent-accessible product with built-in spend controls.

01

Create a meter

Choose a route, MCP tool, or data feed. Set price, unit, customer rules, and allowed payment assets.

02

Attach policies

Operators connect a wallet, issue a metered client key, and define spend caps, allowlists, and expiration windows.

03

Record every receipt

Meterflow ties payment proof to the request, updates analytics, attributes provider revenue, and exports receipts.
