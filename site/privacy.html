Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-05-10

What we store

Meterflow stores the minimum data required to meter, settle, and audit paid API usage:

  • Wallet address. The public Solana address you connect.
  • Metered keys. Hashed API keys you issue for your agents.
  • Request receipts. Endpoint, unit, price, response status, and policy result for each paid call.
  • Budget policies. The caps, allowlists, and revocation rules you configure.

What we do not collect

We do not collect personal information beyond your wallet address. We do not request name, email, government ID, or payment cards. We do not read message bodies that pass through proxied AI routes for training or analytics.

Cookies and tracking

Meterflow does not use third-party tracking cookies. Session state is stored in your browser's session storage and tied only to your wallet identifier.

Data retention

Receipts are retained for 30 days for free-tier wallets and longer windows for utility-token holders. You can delete a metered key at any time via the dashboard, which severs future usage from that client.

Third parties

Meterflow runs on Solana. On-chain settlements are public by nature. We use the following infrastructure providers and surface their metadata as integrations: Helius (RPC), Jupiter (routing), Phantom (wallet), CoinGecko (market data), Discord (operator alerts).
